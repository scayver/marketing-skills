# Sales Engagement

## 1. outreach

**Type:** cli
**Category:** Sales Engagement
**Platform:** LinkedIn / X

**Hook**
`outreach.js` turns agent advice into executable marketing ops.

**Post**
This zero-dependency Node.js CLI gives your agent a practical helper for sales engagement command-line helper for Outreach. It outputs JSON, supports scriptable workflows, and fits Claude Code, OpenAI Codex, Cursor, and Windsurf projects where marketing work needs real platform actions instead of another checklist.

**Install**
