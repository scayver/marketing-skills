## SaaS Launch Stack

Build a SaaS launch stack inside your AI agent.

Use Marketing Skills OS as the operating system: `product-marketing`, `launch`, `landing-page-cro`, `email-marketing`, and `linkedin-strategy` for the workflow. Pair them with integration guides like HubSpot, Stripe, GitHub, and GA4. Add CLI helpers when you need structured data or repeatable execution.

Install: `npx skills add scayver/marketing-skills`
