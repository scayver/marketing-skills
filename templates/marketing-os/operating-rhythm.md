# Operating Rhythm

## Daily

- Check active campaign status.
- Capture customer signals.
- Ship one small asset or improvement.

## Weekly

- Update the scoreboard.
- Review active experiments.
- Choose one primary workflow.
- Publish or ship campaign assets.
- Capture learnings and update context.

## Monthly

- Review channel performance.
- Refresh ICP and positioning.
- Prune stale experiments.
- Pick the next strategic bet.

## Quarterly

- Revisit category, offer, and growth model.
- Decide whether to scale, fix, pause, or replace each channel.

## Agent prompt

```text
Use marketing-os. Run the weekly operating rhythm: review context, scorecard, experiments, learning log, and campaign assets. Produce next week's plan.
```
